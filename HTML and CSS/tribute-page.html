Sandeep Lamichhane

A passionate Cricketer from Nepal

Sandeep Lamichhane (Nepali: सन्दीप लामिछाने; born 2 August 2000) is a Nepalese cricketer who plays for the Nepalese national team. A right-handed leg spin bowler, Lamichhane has played for Twenty20 franchise sides across the world, including for the Delhi Capitals in the Indian Premier League (IPL), Melbourne Stars in the Big Bash League (BBL) and Sylhet Sixers in the Bangladesh Premier League (BPL).

He made his T20 international debut in a Twenty20 International (T20I) match against the West Indies, where he represented a World XI side. He became the first cricketer to make a T20 international debut representing a combined team.[1] He was one of the eleven cricketers to play in Nepal's first One Day International (ODI) match, against the Netherlands, in August 2018
